About

Want to stop saying certain words? Use Newspeak, which counts and beeps each time you say a word you don't want to say!

Newspeak helps you gain awareness of your speaking habits, so you can make the changes you want. When Newspeak is on, you'll hear a beep whenever the program hears a word from your blocklist. It also keeps track of how many times you say a word from your blocklist.

Private

No audio transcripts are ever sent to us, and we use the built-in speech recognition system on your phone.

Free

Newspeak is completely free, with no in-app purchases and no ads!

Intuitive

Newspeak revolves around a central dashboard, an all-in-one overview of your words.

Features

Blocklists

Use Newspeak's presets (swear words/offensive words) or easily create custom word lists!

Friend Mode

Prank your friends by putting Newspeak into Friend Mode! When Friend Mode is on, your phone will appear to be asleep, but will beep when your friend says a word from your blocklist.
